💧 Kendall County Texas Water Table

Cow Creek GCD groundwater monitoring network · Kendall County, Texas · Live data from the Texas Water Development Board.
How to read this: every reading is feet below the ground — how far down you'd have to go to reach the top of the water. A bigger number means the water has dropped deeper underground and there's less of it left in the aquifer. It measures the depth to the water, not how deep the well is or how much water remains.
